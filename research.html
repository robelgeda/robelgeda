Research

    The research teams at Rutgers have given me an opportunity to explore the universe at large and microscopic scales. I am mainly working with two teams at Rutgers with physics backgrounds but very different research applications. I have been working under professor Alyson Brooks on dark matter simulations. I am currently developing software that can acutely track dark matter halos according to their mass. The Rutgers Laser Spectroscopy Laboratory explores the quantum mechanics behind crystal structures using Raman spectroscopy. I have been working on software to improve data analysis procedures and will continue to do experiments in the near future. I have included descriptions of projects I have been involved with.

Dark Matter Simulations

The team under professor Alyson Brooks performs analysis on data from dark matter halo simulations. The current interests of the team include the role irregular galaxies play on the formation of Milky Way mass galaxies. This can be done by closely studying the dark matter halo associated with the galaxies and how they merge to form a large structure. The simulations take into account variables such as supernovae and star formation.


Sort galaxy formation and evolution simulation by Princeton Astrophysics
My task has been to develop software, mostly written in IDL, to track the dark matter halos as they progress through various time steps. So far we have been able to produce results with integrity and plan on improving them. I am currently using Mathematica code (Wolfram language) to plot and display the path taken by the galaxies. I have had the liberty of developing this procedure with a large degree of freedom under the close supervision of Dr. Brooks. Since the final results are so resent, I will be able to share them soon after various checks and presentations.

What Rutgers has to say

Rutgers, The State University of New Jersey, is a leading national research university and the state of New Jersey's preeminent, comprehensive public institution of higher education. Established in 1766 and celebrating a milestone 250th anniversary in 2016, the university is the eighth-oldest higher education institution in the United States. More than 65,000 students and 24,000 faculty and staff learn, work, and serve the public at Rutgers locations across New Jersey and around the world.
